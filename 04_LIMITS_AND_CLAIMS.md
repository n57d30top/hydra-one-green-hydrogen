# Limits And Safe Claims

## Safe Claims

- Hydra One screened 24 bounded catalyst candidates in an advisory-only discovery lane.
- The packet identifies a top-ranked subset for scientific discussion.
- The packet includes deterministic surrogate structure artifacts for four candidates.
- The artifact chain is inspectable and computationally reproducible.

## Unsafe Claims

- experimentally confirmed crystal structures
- synthesis-ready catalyst recipes
- wet-lab validated electrochemical performance
- partner-lab readiness
- deployment or commercialization readiness

## Why The Distinction Matters

The package is strongest as a reviewable computational research packet. It is intentionally weaker than a publication-quality or experimentally validated materials claim. That honesty is a feature, not a defect, because it tells the reviewer exactly what they are evaluating.
